## Implemented Fruchterman-Reingold algorithm for map generation
**Project:**

[space-invasion](https://github.com/sammy-hutch/space-invasion)


**Details:** 

Wanted to generate a map similar to [Spirit Island boards](https://spiritislandwiki.com/index.php?title=Island_Boards), where there are zones of irregular shape with boundaries to other zones. 

Decided to model this as a graph of nodes, partly to make modelling easier, and partly because for the sci-fi theme of this project the graph & node structure would look good for solar systems and interplanetary lines of travel.
