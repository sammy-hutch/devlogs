## New Project, and revolving planet sprite!
**Project:**

[pixelart_planet_maker](https://github.com/sammy-hutch/pixelart_planet_maker)


**Details:** 

initiated a new project, pixelart_planet_maker, which uses python to procedurally generate planet pixelart sprites. 

The planets are modeled as a series of points on the surface of the sphere. these points are painted, and then mapped to a 2D image. The sphere can be rotated and a new image generated, creating a gif from a series of images.

![planet_gif](/devlogs/images/2026-04-20_planet.gif)

The tool will have functionality for customising different colors and patterns for the planets, as well as adding ice caps, clouds and rings
